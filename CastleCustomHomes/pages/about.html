Our Story

Meet the
Castle Family

Castle's Custom Homes is more than a builder—it's a family-owned business built on relationships, integrity, and a deep love for the Magic Valley.

Who We Are

A Builder You Can Trust

Since opening our doors, Castle's Custom Homes has been defined by one thing above all else: doing right by our clients. Kyle and Tammy Castle built this company with the conviction that homebuilding should be honest, personal, and rewarding—for the builder and the family moving in.

Kyle Castle

Co-Founder, Design & Construction

Kyle oversees every build from the ground up—literally. With years of hands-on construction experience across the Magic Valley, he brings both technical precision and creative vision to every project. Kyle is your primary point of contact from design through completion.

What Drives Us

Our Values

I

Integrity

We say what we mean and do what we say. No surprises, no runarounds. Honest pricing and honest communication from day one.

C

Craftsmanship

We take pride in every nail, every joint, every finish. Your home deserves to be built as well as it can possibly be built.

C

Community

Twin Falls and the Magic Valley are our home too. We hire locally, support locally, and are invested in the health of this community.

P

Partnership

We treat every client like a long-term partner. Our relationship doesn't end when you get the keys—we're here whenever you need us.

Community

Rooted in the Magic Valley

Beyond building homes, Castle's Corner Corp supports local events, youth programs, and the broader Twin Falls business community. When you build with Castle's, you're investing in a company that invests right back in your neighborhood.
